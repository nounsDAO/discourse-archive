Bug report for nouns subgraph and nouns agora

Pablo

While trying to implement EIP-4824 for Nouns DAO, we found that there are inconsistent results obtained from subgraph, nouns agora, and on-chain query.

Taking 0xfa4fc4ec2f81a4897743c5b4f45907c02ce06199 as an example, results from two nouns subgraph are 25, and result from nouns agora is 14. However, the correct on chain result is 18 (can be checked with etherscan and opensea).

I believe that basic data accuracy is super important for any governance and DApp based on Nouns. Thus, we reach out here to see if anyone who manages nouns subgraph or nouns agora can help with this issue.
