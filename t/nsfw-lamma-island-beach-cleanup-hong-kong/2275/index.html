NSFW: Lamma Island Beach Cleanup (Hong Kong)

Josan

Hi Nouns community!

TL;DR: a community beach cleanup on Lamma island “Dogs beach” to redistribute some ETH based on the amount of trash collected. Create some teams with kids and grown-ups and have fun picking up trash.

There was a big typhoon late August and the beach is a bit of a mess with debris everywhere. It is a family beach where we leave a lot of gears (canoe, boats, paddle boards etc) and it is very lively on the week end, We would get a lot of cool media content out of it, photos and videos (drone shot, group shots etc).

WhatsApp Image 2022-09-09 at 10.51.05 AM1600×1200 292 KB

We would like to make two groups: “Big items” and “Micro plastics”.

On top of that we want to educate people about crypto and nouns and help them setting up wallets.

We would like to ask for 1.5 ETH:

  • 1 ETH to distribute to the community with a cleaning game with ETH as a reward. We would like to distribute it as much as possible.
  • 0.5 ETH organize the cleanup: rent a vehicle to get the trash to the collection point, refreshments, and media costs.

WhatsApp Image 2022-09-09 at 10.54.48 AM1023×576 142 KB

Its a cool place with a hippy vibe and lot of nationalities represented (also called dogs beach). Nounish for sure!

Who we are: @Josan_studio/@greennouns and @Coralorca .
