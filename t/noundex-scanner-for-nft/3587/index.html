Noundex - scanner for NFT

BETEPAH

Introduction:

We propose the creation of an NFT Pokedex application, a tool that allows users to keep track of their NFT collections, similar to how a Pokedex allows users to keep track of their Pokemon collections. The application will have several key features such as a catalog, search, portfolio, scanner, market, and social features.

It will be accessible through a mobile device and web platforms, and it will offer different levels of access, such as a free version that allows users to access basic features and a paid version that allows them to access advanced features such as market data and portfolio analysis.

Noundex1920×1080 207 KB

Key Features:

  • Catalog: A database of all the NFTs that the user owns, including information such as the name, image, and metadata of the NFT, as well as its current market value.

  • Search: A feature that allows users to search for specific NFTs within their collection using keywords, categories, or other criteria.

  • Portfolio: A feature that allows users to view their entire NFT collection in one place, with the ability to organize and group NFTs by different criteria, such as series, artist, or rarity.

  • Scanner: A feature that uses the device’s camera to scan QR codes on NFTs, making it easy for users to add new NFTs to their collection.

  • Market: A feature that allows users to buy and sell NFTs on different marketplaces, and track their sales and purchases history.

  • Social: A feature that allows users to connect with other NFT collectors, share their collection, and discover new NFTs.

Target Audience: The target audience for this application is NFT collectors and enthusiasts, who are looking for a tool to keep track of their collections and discover new NFTs.

Development Plan:

  • Research and analysis of the current market and competitors
  • Design and development of the application
  • Testing and quality assurance
  • Deployment and launch
  • Ongoing maintenance and updates

Development Team: Our development team will consist of experienced developers, designers, and project managers, with a proven track record of delivering successful mobile and web applications.

Budget: The budget for this project is 20 ETH, which is roughly equivalent to $30,000. This budget would cover the costs of creating and promoting the application, with some room for contingencies or unexpected expenses.

Conclusion: We believe that this application will be a valuable tool for NFT collectors and enthusiasts, and we are confident that our team has the skills and experience to deliver a high-quality product. We look forward to the opportunity to bring this project to life and contribute to the growing NFT community.
