Proposal: Nouns Chocolate

TL;DR: Proofing a batch of 300 bars of artisan chocolate - Nouns Chocolate

Description: To continue and strive to make Nouns into a well known branding throughout the world and to introduce Nouns branding to more Singapore community, I would like to proposed the creation of Nouns Chocolate; the latest nounish consumable after Nouns Coffee, Nouns Baked Beans & Nouns Dust (BBQ Rub)

Why Chocolate?: The answer is why not? Chocolate is the most widely and frequently craved food and its simply delicious, not to mention all its health benefits ranging from protecting our cells from inflammation, boost our immune system etc. Nouns Chocolate is NOUNISH! ⌐◨-◨

Phase 1 - Pilot:

  • For this pilot phase of Nouns Chocolate, high-quality artisan will be sourced to white-label for Nouns Chocolate branding.
  • All the chocolate will be made from fine quality cocoa from reliable producers, who make effort to take care of their cocoa trees, harvest the pods only when its ripe and ferment the seeds properly to allow flavor development.
  • Although this is a pilot, its important to care and ensure the quality of the product put out under Nouns branding as well as the consumer who will be consuming the product. Consumers are able to taste the quality.
  • Work with artisan on the recipe and Nouns Chocolate packaging

Sales - Ecommerce:

  • To streamline the operation and simplify the purchasing process, for this pilot phase sales will be via ecommerce platform.
  • Sale of Nouns Chocolate will be made available locally, across various e-commerce platforms that are widely used by citizens of Singapore (Shopee, Lazada, shopify, Amazon)
  • 100% of proceed from this pilot phase will be converted to ETH and send back to SG/NSFW or used to front run portion of Phase 2 depending on demand.

Advertising / Marketing:

  • Advertisement will be put up across the various e-commerce platforms to have Nouns Chocolate featured/search advertisement.
  • Marketing via social media, engaging food bloggers and influencer to promote Nouns Chocolate.
  • Instill word of mouth by adding a more personal touch to buyer’s experience; all Nouns Chocolate will be hand delivered along with thank you card that double ups as a information card with regards to NounsDAO. By providing hand delivery instead of engaging courier services, this serve as a form of OTG activation as well.

Phase 2:

  • With the success execution of Phase 1, base on the demand for Nouns Chocolate, a second proposal for expansion will be submitted on-chain.
  • The second proposal will focus on expanding Nouns Chocolate production, study suitable packaging for long distance and introduce oversea shipping, setting up of Nouns Chocolate website with the integration for token gating for Nouners and Nounders to have special perks when purchasing Nouns Chocolate, expanding the team as well as brand building and brand identity.
