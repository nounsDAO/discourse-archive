Nouns X Internet Game: Season 2 w/ gmoney

lej

Dear NounsDAO,

I’m a huge fans of Nouns. A big part of my Solidity education was forking the Nouns contract back when we were at Noun #3. So I’m super thrilled to present an idea that integrates Nouns into what I’ve been working on for 2 years, & can contribute to the Nounish goal of spreading the Nouns meme far & wide.

TLDR: We are Internet Game. We host massive web3 game shows with thousands of players and blue-chip NFT prizes. Our Season 2 is coming up next, hosted by gmoney, and we’d like to re-skin one of the games to feature Nouns, as well as educate our players about Nouns with a video prior to the game.

Before we get into specifics, we’ll share a bit more about who we are and why we’re qualified to propose this…

What is Internet Game??

Internet Game is Squid Game meets NFTs. We create live, interactive game shows that anyone can play at home with hyper-simple, yet dangerously fun mini-games. 60% of buy-ins go to blue-chip NFT prizes, all voted on by the community in Discord.

In March 2022 we launched our Internet Game: Season 1. 8000 players minted $2.2M in NFTs to play. We then purchased $1M+ in NFT prizes as voted on by the players, including Bored Apes, Mutant Apes, Doodles, Azukis, CloneX and many more.

The best way to fully understand how our seasons work is to look at this case study.

Internet Game: Season 2 “Bear Market Battle” launches on September 12th. Season 2 features 6 brand new games taking place between September 12th and 23rd. The entire event is hosted by the infamous gmoney & comedian Leah Lamarr. Gmoney will appear as a full 3D version of his cryptopunk by being rigged up in a high-fidelity motion capture suit, and will then be superimposed on an XR stage (picture a gigantic LED screen & floor) next to Leah.

The Opportunity: Nouns Game + Video in Season 2

Internet Game Season 2 is made up of 6 mini-games that take place over the course of two weeks. Each game runs for 24 hours.

There is an opportunity to fully brand the third game, currently titled as “The Last Spaceship”, and transform it into “The Last Noun”.

The game itself is similar to an old school Asteroids / Galaga space shooter, but with an original take on how you move around the space. We think the 2D arcade aesthetic is a perfect match for the Nouns art. Both the main character as well as the enemies would be Nouns:

1600×900 132 KB

After the completion of Season 2, “The Last Noun” will exist on the Internet Game Arcade (launching Q1 2023) and used in future tournaments with other Discords, influencers and NFT communities, bringing consistent exposure to the brand to a variety of audiences.

The Video

A video is shown before each of the games featuring gmoney & Leah. If desired, we could include a trailer / explainer / hype video for Nouns in that video. This would need to be provided by Nouns DAO.

Additional opportunities during Season 2:

  • Including Nouns and/or Lil Nouns in our prize pool
  • Skinning the entire Internet Game tournament page during “The Last Noun” game to be Nouns-branded
  • Upon the launch of the game, post a write up on our social platforms (Twitter/Discord) to educate our audience on Nouns DAO and the Nouns mission & mechanics

Our Ask:

ETH Sponsorship: Nouns DAO would contribute 20 ETH to Internet Game Season 2. The sponsorship would be split to further contribute to the prize pool and cover operational expenditures incurred by Internet Game.

Proposed Timeline & Next Steps

  • Engage in discussions to answer any questions derived from this proposal
  • Gain approval to transform “The Last Spaceship” into “The Last Noun”
  • Announce partnership with Nouns DAO for Season 2 including the donation to the prize pool
  • Complete redesign of the tournament page and game and showcase to Nouns DAO for feedback
  • Launch “The Last Noun” Game on September 16th, along with the optional pre-game video

Long-Term Opportunity: Nouns Custom Casual Game

We we would love to continue the relationship and build a fully custom game for Nouns that can live on in perpetuity. The game would be co-created by the DAO and Internet Game to provide a unique experience that allows consumers to connect with the Nouns brand in an interactive way. Casual gaming is a juggernaut of an industry due to its accessibility (ease of learning), low effort requirement, and repeat value (easy to hop in and out of) it provides. We see a Nouns Game as a way to engage the general population in a highly scalable and interactive way all around the world. Details on this longer-term partnership opportunity can be submitted through a separate proposal.

We would love to hear your thoughts on this proposal!

60% of the funds made with the mint are used to buy NFT prizes. What happens with the 40% left?

Unfortunately not. We have hard data that our players are either not aware or not currently fans of Nouns. A video is shown before the game to all players. If we’re able to show a video explaining Nouns or even just a little “teaser trailer”, then all of our players will become more deeply aware of Nouns. On top of that, they will then play a game featuring the the Nouns. Marketing is all about making people see your brand 10,000 times. This will help Nouns get closer to that with a fairly hardcore but more mainstream NFT audience.
