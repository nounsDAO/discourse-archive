Sailing PR campaign, Korea Blockchain Week 2022 [6,7 August]

fiveoceans_dev

TLTR

Display Nouns glasses on 5 sailboats in the middle of Seoul during Korea Blockchain Week 2022.

Description

  • Sail 40 km on five J/24 sailboats with a huge Nouns themed sails.
  • Location: Hangang river, Seoul, South Korea (population 15 million)
  • The river is surrounded with two 20 km highway roads from International Airport to Center of Seoul.
  • photos and videos to promote NounsDAO during Korea Blockchain Week.

Description


J/24 sailboat is the most famous sailboat model of all time! I propose to display nouns glasses on 5 sailboats in the middle of Seoul, South Korea. Seoul is home to 15+ million people and a lot of interest towards blockchain and NFTs among the population. The Korean government, businesses, and people are interested in blockchain and the NFT space. Hangang river is surrounded by two highways and will be a perfect place to reach lots of people during rush hours. The two way distance will be around 40+ kilometers. This could be a great opportunity to advertise NounsDAO and attract potential future members and developers.

I personally know lots of sailors who want to participate in the Nouns Seoul PR campaign. We will create a fleet of five J/24 sailboats and sail along the river from Kimpo international airport to Itaewon bridge and back.

Spinnaker sail designs

I have connections to Hyde sails dealership and can place an expedited order for 5 spinnaker sails with printed logo of nouns glasses.
