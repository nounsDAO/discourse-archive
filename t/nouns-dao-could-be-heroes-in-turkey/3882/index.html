Hello dear Nouns DAO, as you may have heard from the news, a catastrophic 7.8 magnitude earthquake struck Turkey & Syria on Feb 6 – the worst natural disaster in the region since 1939. We need NounsDAO’s help, our country has been in mourning for days and the earthquake affected 10 cities, millions of people were left homeless and (as far as is now known) tens of thousands of people died.
