Tech Grants Pod: Szn 2 Ideas and Priorities

verb-e

TLDR: help prioritize work for the tech pod’s next ~3-month szn.

Szn 1 is coming to a close, with descriptor v2 going live, and DAO v2 about to go into audit. We’re kicking off a new szn 2 of protocol / infrastructure improvements, and we’re asking for your help in coming up with ideas, and then prioritizing the best ones.

Ideas so far

These are just floating ideas, nothing set in stone; please jump in and express your likes and dislikes, and any new ideas or problems you’d like to solve.

  • Governance

    • [] Defend against last-minute vote swings (e.g. lido’s two-phase voting)
    • [] Ragequit w/ dynamic penalty
    • [] Granular vote delegation (without manually moving Nouns to separate wallets)
    • Dialectic has two posts on Nouns governance: 1, 2

  • Financial mechanisms

    • [] Allow the DAO to execute proposals that pay builders a predictable amount of stablecoin, using a ETH/stablecoin price oracle (e.g. ChainLink) at the moment of execution (to remove the proposal timeline ETH volatility risk)
    • [] An on-chain Nouns price feed / oracle
    • [] Noun lending / buyback
    • [] Improve approach used by the DAO to acquire nouns (for distribution to builders, etc)

  • Treasury management

    • [] Research how the DAO can DCA (dollar-cost average) into various tokens

  • Gas optimizations

    • [] Auction house gas optimizations; can reduce settlement and bid creation gas quite a bit (maybe good to complete if we’re already in there someday)

davidbrai

Adding a few thoughts regarding the “on-chain nouns price feed / oracle”:

The way it could work is by storing each auction result in the auction house contract, including the price at which the auction closed.
Having an on-chain oracle for the prices of Nouns can open up a lot of potential DeFi use cases. The one that immediately comes to mind is for enabling using Nouns as a collateral for lending protocols.
Given that it’s a relatively simple addition, I think it can open a lot of interesting potential integrations for Nouns.
