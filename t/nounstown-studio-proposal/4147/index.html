The opportunity

1600×840 59 KB

Our proposal is to bring the fascinating world of nouns to life through an array of engaging content on social media platforms.

Our vision is to deliver daily doses of memes, weekly news updates, and other captivating content that is both entertaining and informative. Imagine scrolling through your Instagram feed and stumbling upon a hilarious meme that perfectly captures the essence of a noun, or receiving weekly news updates in a creative way that keeps you in the loop about the latest nouns-related happenings or even macro events.

But wait, there’s more! We are also excited to introduce a series of original comics that delve into the rich history of nouns. These comics will be a fun and unique way for members to discover the origins and evolution of different nouns and their cultural significance.

This is a unique opportunity to not only increase engagement within the community but also to expand the reach of NounsDAO beyond the blockchain space.

Work Recap

Nounstown Studio has embarked on a creative venture by producing daily comic strips that are regularly uploaded on our social media channels. The primary objective of these comic strips is to enhance public awareness of Nounstown Studio and its Noun characters by weaving captivating narratives around them. To cater to a wider audience, we create comics in two languages, Indonesian and English, and endeavor to upload two strips per day.

Our comics have also been featured on various platforms to showcase our artwork to a wider audience. For instance, we have partnered with Webtoon Indonesia to produce a comic series that highlights Noun characters and their adventures. Additionally, our website serves as a hub for our comic uploads.

We have plans to expand our reach through strategic marketing. Currently, we are identifying key opinion leaders who align with Nounstown Studio’s vision and mission to amplify our content on social media and Webtoon Indonesia. Furthermore, we have initiated digital marketing efforts such as Instagram Ads to promote our daily comic strips on Instagram. Lastly, we intend to publish printed comics in conjunction with Noun character merchandise.

The team

At the heart of our team lies a master illustrator and a seasoned story writer, whose combined expertise fuels our creative engine. Recognizing the vast scope of our ambitions, we’ve also made the astute decision to bring on board a skilled story writer, thus freeing up our illustrators to focus on their dazzling artwork.

Moreover, we’ve recruited a team of social media experts who will assist us in executing a targeted digital marketing program. Our social media specialists are adept at crafting strategies that will increase public awareness during the rollout of Nounstown Studio’s content.

At Nounstown Studio, we take pride in the caliber of our core team. Each member brings with them a wealth of experience and a deep understanding of the creative processes that drive our projects. From ideation to execution, our team is equipped with the skills necessary to bring our vision to life in a manner that is truly extraordinary.

Our KPIs for success

To ensure that we are delivering the most engaging and entertaining content possible, we will be closely monitoring key performance indicators (KPIs) to measure engagement and community growth.

Our social media team will be keeping a watchful eye on metrics such as follower count, likes, comments, shares, and overall engagement to gauge the level of interest and involvement within the NounsDAO community. We will also be tracking website traffic and user behavior to identify opportunities for optimization and enhance the user experience.

But that’s not all - we are also excited to see what creative ideas the community will bring to the table. By monitoring user-generated content related to our content, such as memes or fan art, we can see firsthand the impact that our content is having on the community and identify areas for improvement.

Community involvement is at the heart of our mission, which is why we will also be tracking participation in community events and discussions. We want to create a space where members feel empowered to contribute their ideas, share their experiences, and connect with one another.

To make this journey happen, we need the funding to bring our vision to life, to create a community that is inspired, engaged, and curious.
