Nouns Hack Week - Nounify a City (Seeking Feedback and Thoughts)

GeObts

Hi everyone,

I am excited to have presented a proposal to proliferate NounsDAO in Playa Del Carmen through by incorporating NounsDAO branding into local businesses and providing marketing materials. As an avid street artist and NounsDAO enthusiast, I believe this is a great opportunity to combine my passion for art with the promotion of the NounsDAO brand.

I would love and I am open to any/all feedback and suggestions from the community and would appreciate your thoughts on this proposal. If by any chance I am given the vote of confidence, I will use this as well as my Twitter to share updates and the progress of the project.

Thank you for your time and consideration, I look forward to hearing from you.
