[Proposal] Nouns x OG - Nouns on every NFT

Kieran

TL;DR

  • This proposal is for NounsDAO to fund an exciting collaboration between Nouns and OG, to create an exclusive Nouns x OG Frame. And make every NFT in the world become more nounish.
  • This proposal includes the production, development and distribution costs for 100 Nouns x OG Frames and the OG App.
  • Each Nouns x OG Frame will retail for 1Ξ.
  • Total proposed cost = 280Ξ.
  • The Nouns x OG collection will be available for purchase on the OG website via ETH. For all online orders, production and shipment will be handled through OG.
  • The Nouns x OG drop will feature on the OG website and multiple media channels.
  • Every Nouner will be eligible for 10% discount. Also, Nouns DAO will receive 20% revenue share per completed order. (Only applicable to the 100 units of the Limited Edition Nouns x OG Frame that are produced.)

OG Frame

Want to spread the word on Nouns and proudly display your Nouns ownership? Meet the OG Frame.

  • First class display: Stunning colors and a vivid, sharp full HD display.
  • The perfect fit for your PFPs/NFTs: Engineered to fit the most popular artworks created by some of the web’s most prolific NFT artists.
  • From wallet to wall: From your Metamask wallet, use your smartphone to safely cast your NFTs.

Nouns on Every NFT

Bring Nouns to life with OG. Nouns x OG isn’t just a frame that displays your art and NFTs. It’s a proud representation of the true ownership of the NFT, exclusive to Nouners.

Only 100 x OG Frames will be produced under the Nouns x OG collaboration. Every Nouner will be eligible for 10% discount and Nouns DAO will receive 20% revenue share per completed order.

We’ll create a limited edition one-off design for this project. The Nouns x OG Frame will have the Nouns logo, and the frame will has the color based on your Nouns glasses color.

Download the OG App and use the Nouns Glasses Filter on any of your NFTs. Customize the color, size, and position of your Noun Glasses and instantly display your customized NFTs on your OG Frame.

Nouns x OG Frame will retail for 1Ξ.

Your NFTs Only

Unlike other digital frames on the market today, the Nouns x OG Frame will present a truly exclusive and personal display. Only Nouners who are the actual current owners of the NFT can make the NFT appear on their Nouns x OG Frame display.

app-layout1920×1080 103 KB

Following tests and prototypes, we determined the size of the OG Frame that is best suited for your PFPs/NFTs (20”L x 20”W x 2.7”H x 20 lbs).

We’ll also build an OG App, which will allow Nouners to easily connect to their crypto wallet. The OG App is now linked to many blockchains, ensuring you can showcase your Nouns and your favorite NFTs on all connected products from OG.

When you own an OG Frame, the photos, artworks, and NFTs that are displayed are NFTs that are owned and displayed by you, and you alone. Every OG Frame is completely unique, and exactly what you, the creator, makes of it. The power of expression is yours!

OG Frames are available in a variety of premium finishes based on your Nouns glasses color.

Why we’re doing this

  • We love and believe in the real value of what Nouns are bringing to the world right now. The Nouns community makes us want to be a part of the action. We want to be a part of the movement.
  • We want to help spread the word on Nouns, encourage more NFT ownerships, and increase awareness to non-crypto natives across the globe.
  • To hit the ground running and start this exciting journey with a bang. We plan to build and expand the OG ecosystem. This is just the beginning.

Benefits for Nouns

  • IRL revenue share.
  • Grow the number of people that know about Nouns.
  • A new way to reach other NFT communities and let them know about Nouns.
  • Future benefits from OG.

Timeline

  • Phase 1 (September): Design, Tests, and Development.
  • Phase 2 (2nd week October): Further developments and improvements following feedback on Nouns’ Discord and Discourse.
  • Phase 3 (3rd week October): Submit proposal.
  • Phase 4 (October - November): Production.
  • Phase 5 (December 2022 - February 2023): Sales, Processing, and Shipping.
