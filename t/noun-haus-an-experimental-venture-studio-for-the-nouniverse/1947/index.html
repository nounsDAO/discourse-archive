TL;DR

An experimental venture studio for the Nouniverse, ready to fund and spin up powerful Nounish models that will target domain specific applications.

Background

Proposal 51 rewarded the extremely nounish Gnars DAO with 69 ETH to sponsor Nouns Athletes after a 3 ETH proof of concept funded by Small Grants, which along with a myriad of other nounish experiments, informs this proposal and its approach in solving for proliferation (as opposed to propagation).

0 → 1 → N

Nouns DAO exists to facilitate a virtuous cycle of “proliferation” and yet despite our success, most of what we have funded is in fact “propagation.” This is most likely a function of first needing to occupy our own space (propagate) before we can divide and conquer (proliferate).

As nouns the difference between propagation and proliferation is that propagation is the multiplication or natural increase in a population while proliferation is (uncountable) the process by which an organism produces others of its kind; breeding, propagation, procreation, reproduction.

Small Grants was formed to facilitate the issuance of smaller grants. By spreading our bets across a broader subset of opportunities we’ve accelerated propagation. In some cases we’ve enabled early examples of proliferation by funding various proof of concept proposals as a “validation” step.

NOUN HAUS is an alternative to Small Grants who maximise daotput by empowering communities, artists and entrepreneurs to proliferate Nouns as domain specific extensions – from zero to one to n.

0 → 1 → N

  • 0 = validate
  • 1 = propagate
  • N = proliferate

Inspired by Bauhaus and akin to having an ‘entreprenouner’ in residence, NOUN HAUS is well poised for the arrival of Nouns Builder and is testament to what Noun 22 said on Bankless when you consider the impending onslaught of nounish extensions:

I think what’s critical to the future of scaling the dao […] is finding other ways to do small grants programs that get one to two ETH into the hands of as many talented builders as we can and then use that as a way to onboard them to the dao and then scale up their contributions…

A key insight gained through our six months of full-time (and capital) dedication to Nouns is that smaller sized grants VALIDATE so builders can PROPAGATE; but it’s extensions that PROLIFERATE ⌐◨-◨
