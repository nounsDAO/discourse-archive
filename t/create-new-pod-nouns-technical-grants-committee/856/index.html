Create new pod: Nouns Technical Grants Committee

vapeape

NOUNS TECHNICAL GRANTS COMMITTEE

Members:

  • Willprice
  • Vapeape
  • Solimander
  • Brennen

Mission:

  • Prioritize, fund, and mentor projects that committee members deemed to be of technical interest to the DAO
  • Free up Nouns technical resources to be used in a higher level, architectural/managerial role
  • Solicit and incorporate feedback from other DAO members
  • Share periodic updates with the community

Successful Outcomes:

  • Bring fully fledged technical improvements to the DAO for vetting/revision/approval
  • R&D / experimentation should be broadly scoped enough to have failures as well as successes

Initial budget request:

  • 100 ETH to be spent primarily on smart contract design / testing / audits at the discretion of the committee

Genesis Project ideas:

  • A more robust Descriptor contract to allow easy addition/removal
  • Research, prototype, and audit potential financial mechanisms
    • Noun lending, ragequit, Noun buybacks
  • Dynamic governance quorum
  • Research emergency mechanisms that could replace the centralized veto

Structure:

  • 3 of 4 Gnosis Safe for fund custody (Gnosis Safe)
  • 3 of 4 to approve funding decisions
  • All related work will be tracked in Github

Some of the things I would like to see looked into:

  1. expanding governance model using either a fractionalization of nouns to create smaller delegated tokens or through a minted vote token only used to delegate vote power to noun holders, but can be minted independently from nouns. (I would name these vote tokens verbs)

  2. Investigation methods on proposals to challenge outcomes or fund usage once they have been delivered. These challenges have cost to the party, and some punishment to the offending party. (this goes really well with the above vote tokens)

  3. A potential better model for the daily auction bidding, First-price sealed-bid vs. Dutch Auction etc. We should maximize the amount each noun should go for, but not at the cost of having to keep bidding a new price to reset the clock every time (1am central )
