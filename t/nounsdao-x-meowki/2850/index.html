Greetings Andrew!

Initially, I planned to involve various NFT projects and their communities in joint online and offline charitable activities. The idea of a poker tournament to support blindness people is as follows:

• I’m putting together a list of projects that want to participate;
• We gather a general group chat and discuss what the buy-in will be (for example, those who want to participate from a certain community donate 5-10$ for their project’s representative). Also, if desired, projects allocate as prizes to participants NFTs from their collection, e.g. Having begun to build a structure, we together with other projects attract more people to the tournament, who, for example, are not involved in any of the participating projects, but also want to take part;
• At the end of the tournament, the winners receive their prizes, and the official representatives of the participating projects send the funds collected from the tournament to the requisites of the selected charity organisations.

When I started building the project in October I was sure that NFT was more than just hype and art and my ideas would be heard. I was getting support from dozens of big projects at the pre-mint stage (like they were getting WL spots, etc.), but once the project launched, all were gone. I didn’t invest the money in marketing and hype, I sent it to charity for deaf-mute children. I started sending out dozens and hundreds of affiliate offers to various projects, thinking that they would want to participate. And in the end all I get is ignores, rejections and very rarely anyone responds.

I don’t give up, but I have no idea what else to do to be heard and get attention . I’m working out the details.

And in addition I would like to say - I am sure that the participation of various projects in what we do will increase the brand growth of these projects. Yes, it’s not a direct financial offer for the sake of hype, but NFT is about the mood of the community. And if the project community sees that its project is interested in new directions of development - this will allow the project to grow further. So far I have not been able to get these ideas across to many projects, but I am sure that a lot will change in the NFT space this year.
And among other things, I’m sure that as soon as someone famous takes part in the Meowki project - the rest will change their minds and see the prospects in what we do
