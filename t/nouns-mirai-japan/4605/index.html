BACK GROUND

⌐◨-◨ Challenges in Web3

There are common challenges among those involved in Web3. One of the main issues is that there are still many people in the world who are unaware of Web3 or have never encountered it.

In the world of Web2, Facebook has over 3 billion users worldwide, YouTube and WhatsApp have more than 2 billion users, and WeChat and TikTok have over 1 billion users each.

On the other hand, the number of cryptocurrency and Web3 users worldwide is about 320 million, which is only about 4.2% of the global population.

In Japan, it is said that 11% of the population own crypto assets, and there are 20,000 NFT buyers, which is less than 0.02%. This suggests that Web3 has not yet penetrated the mainstream. Naturally, even fewer people are aware of Nouns.

However, we imagine that the growth of the blockchain field and the fact that Nouns and Noggles are IP using CC0 will gradually lead to their widespread adoption, eventually becoming commonplace in society. We believe that participating in the Web3 conference as a timeline to achieve this goal has significant meaning.

⌐◨-◨ PROJECT DETAILS ： Nouns Mirai Japan Booth

⌐◨-◨ TL;DR

At the WebX conference booth, we will introduce visitors to a future where Nouns and Japan are fused together, using a small 3m x 3m x 2.7m space with the spirit of Japanese hospitality, a tea room (exhibition), art (live painting), and a metaverse (technology).

⌐◨-◨ CONCEPT OF BOOTH

  • We anticipate that in the near future (a few years from now), Nouns characters and Noggles will naturally blend into Japanese culture. This place offers visitors a glimpse of that future.

  • We will set up a Japanese-style room in the business conference space, which may seem out of place, and provide an experience where guests can remove their shoes and sit on tatami mats.

  • In the tatami tea room, we will lend Noggle-shaped VR glasses. We will guide each guest’s smartphone to the Japanese-style space “Co-Mati” in the metaverse Spatial, where Nouns Yoko-cho will be set up. Here, we will conduct beginner-friendly Nouns lecture videos and real-time events.

  • An artist will perform live painting inside the booth, and participants will create the future of Nouns together, bringing it to life in a real space.

An illustration envisioning a future where Nouns blend into the streets of Japan in 2-3 years. Noggles become fashionable among trendy high school girls. And soon, the streets will be filled with various colorful Noggles and Nouns.

⌐◨-◨ Nouns-inviting Mirai Trick #1 : To the “Mirai = Future” through “Noren” and “Tea Room”

The anticipation beyond the Japanese hospitality spirit of “tea room” and “noren”

The Japanese spirit of hospitality is based on the modest hospitality of “tea ceremony.” Noren, hung at the storefronts in Japan, also serve not only as a signboard but also as a representation of the store’s credibility and pride. This unique Japanese interior design is expressed in a small 3m x 3m space.

Visitors pass through the noren and are invited into a small yet secluded Japanese space with tatami mats. In this small space, we collaborate with craftsmen who create small tea rooms that can be incorporated into modern life and noren workshops to create an interior design that maximizes the limited space.

This “tea room” will be used as a “portal” connecting to Nouns’ Mirai = “Future.”
