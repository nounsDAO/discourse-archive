Background: YouTube is one of the most popular social media platforms in the world today, with a wide variety of content, including music. However, in the NFT community, there are only a few NFT projects that create music content on the YouTube platform. Therefore, we would like to propose a grant fund proposal to create music cover content on YouTube for the Proliferalation Nouns DAO.

Problem: The NFT community lacks a platform for music content creation, which limits the potential for community engagement and brand recognition.

Introduce Project: We propose to create a YouTube channel for Proliferalation Nouns DAO Community, featuring music cover content. This channel will provide diverse and interesting music content for viewers, help develop the network with musicians and independent labels, establish the Nouns DAO brand as a prominent player in the NFT community, and sustain the project through Adsense revenue and brand sales.

Budget: The total budget for this project is 23 ETH, which will be used for the following:

  • Equipment (Hardware-Software) : 4 ETH
  • Marketing and Collaboration : 5 ETH
  • Brand Development : 4 ETH
  • Team compensation for 6 months : 10 ETH

Marketing:

The budget allocated for marketing and collaboration (5 ETH) will be used to promote the YouTube channel and its content on various social media platforms and through online advertisements. This will include creating and managing social media accounts, creating and distributing promotional materials such as graphics and videos, and conducting online advertising campaigns to reach a wider audience. The marketing budget will also be used to network and collaborate with other musicians and independent labels to expand the reach and diversity of the content on the channel.
