Cointelegraph team is excited to present the draft proposal of a “Week of Nouns” on Cointelegraph aimed at raising the awesome NounsDAO ecosystem to the CT community.

  2. Add Cointelegraph-branded ⌐◨-◨ to all to all Cointelegraph editor’s profile pictures (editors profile included in every article published) [https://cointelegraph.com/about]
