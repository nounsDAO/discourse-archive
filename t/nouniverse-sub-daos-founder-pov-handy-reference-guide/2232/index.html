Nouniverse Sub-DAOs: Founder POV + Handy Reference Guide

Blowned.eth

TLDR;

  • List of all Nouniverse SubDAOs & Extensions on Nouns Center
  • Mega Reference Article on preliminary Sub-DAOs from a Founder POV

Purpose

To craft a handy reference guide for people to find out, learn more, and join various sub-DAOs in the Nounish ecosystem.

This is to:

  • Solve the age-old problem of, “Is there a list of all Nounish subdaos, extensions, etc?”
  • Leave no Nounish sub-dao undiscovered
  • Present sub-daos accurately from a founder’s POV

Deliverables

This project comes in three parts, some segments have already been completed.

This is a comprehensive list of all Nounish Sub-DAOs.

Part 3: Nounish Sub-DAO Mega Article; Founder’s POV

The Mega Article is an in-depth write-up of Nounish Sub-DAOs. From the Sub-DAOs mission and vision, to the unique projects they’re building, and even some little-known fun facts about sub-DAOs, this mega article will talk about just that.

Every Sub-DAO section will be written with the help of interviews from Nounish Sub-DAO founders. Essentially, this info isn’t always written on their front pages.

I’ve created a form for founders and early contributors to fill:

Google Docs

Nounish SubDAO Survey - POV

Answer as naturally as possible for the following questions, and feel free to write to your heart's content! I will take all you have written, add in a little input, and repackage it as a roughly 500 word section. Thanks for helping out!

Finally, I put my own spin on the piece, and some opinions (not too much hopefully), and that’s the work that goes into creating one write-up for a Sub-DAO.

Each section ranges anywhere from 500-800 words, and I’ve already written one for Lil Nouns DAO.

The Future

Of course, this proposal does not cover the vast expanse of the Nouniverse. There will always be some Sub-DAOs which slip through the cracks and are only discovered sometime later. But as we grow, we build.

If this proposal comes to pass, I plan to grow the list even further.

  • Add Sub-DAOs and Nounish extensions to List
  • Write-ups for Nounish extensions on Nouns Center as well
  • More write-ups for Nounish Sub-DAOs and Nounish extensions
  • Maybe a podcast where Nounish founders speak about their project for 1 minute, rapid fire rounds?
  • Should these reference guides be uploaded on Nouns Notion page itself?
