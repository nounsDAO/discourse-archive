Hey, JP here! Everyone loves noggles stickers and I love treasure hunts! So why not put them together?

As my submission to the “Make Nouns Go Viral” round on Prop House, I created a project (in portuguese) called “Find the sticker ⌐◨-◨” where I post videos sticking noggles around and inviting people to find them! Unfortunately, the proposal did not passed, but since I believe it accomplished the challenge and has a lot of potential for keep growing, I am here to ask for funding.

Two accounts were used for testing purposes: encontreoadesivo (which I started from scratch and already have almost 6k followers) and adesivosnouns (a pre-existing account with 82k followers)

I have been a motion designer and content creator for the past ten years and for the last five months I am splitting my time between my job and Nouns more and more. This situation itself is not sustainable because I’m not involved directly in any project that has funding to work for a couple of months but let’s keep going.

I am currently building Nouns in Rio and InternatioNouns. I have worked for Nouns News and NounsBR (Nouns Week). I am building for Nouns DAO for almost five months now and I can easily say that I didn’t receive as payment more than 3 ETH including all contests that I won.

I’m not here for the money. If it was the case, I’m sure I wouldn’t be here anymore. I’m here because five months ago I heard about Nouns and got obsessed with the project and the community. I made a lot of true friends here and I’m here for them too.

Back to the project, here is why it’s not sustainable. Making Nouns go viral wasn’t luck, I worked for that. Keeping a TikTok account running is not just posting a video once or twice a week and it’s done. And I know how to do that. I spent around 3 hours a day to make the MVP for the Prop House round and that’s what I’m planning to do if I get funded. It’s what I believe it’s necessary to go above and beyond with this specific project.

So the project is not sustainable because I don’t have 3 free hours every day to dedicate to it without funding. I had an idea, created an MVP, and presented it to the DAO.
