WTF IS AN ANIMATIC?

An animatic is a preliminary version of a movie, animated short, or episode, produced by shooting successive sections of a storyboard and adding a soundtrack.

So again, WTF is an animatic?

Animatics are used to plan an animation and use low-resolution images to convey timing, rather than the look and feel of the final piece. An animatic is the pre-cursor to the final animation and is a key part of the planning process.

Again we are proposing creating an animatic for this proposal.

We are creating the animatics in 4 phases per short.

  • Scripting
  • Storyboarding
  • Scratch Track Audio
  • Editing
