Noun Sounds: Music POD

SuperTightWoody

Hey all- a TON has been happening behind the scenes at Noun Sounds and we are excited to present our NOUN SOUNDS MUSIC POD PROPOSAL ⌐◨-◨

Mission

After a successful execution of proposal 139, Noun Sounds will build upon it’s current momentum by furthering the build of the platform and introduce a music pod.

Over a 6 month period, our goal is to onboard and empower musicians, build upon the existing Noun Sounds platform, create a DAO structure and develop new systems for musicians and music lovers to create, share and participate in Noun Sounds- building a self-sustaining community around all things music.

Total: 245.5 ETH

(See bottom of proposal for funding breakdown)

Proposal Outline

From January-June 2023, Noun Sounds Pod will:

→ Empower + Onboard Musicians

→ Further Develop Nouns Sounds Platform

→ Soundish Partnerships

→. Create Noun Sounds DAO

Empowering + Onboarding Musicians

image1015×417 75.1 KB

One of our top initiatives is to empower musicians through funding and community building. Our initial conversations include:

  • Large-scale Artist 61ETH

    • Ghostface Killah from Wu-Tang Clan: in discussions about making a CC0 song and sound pack for Noun Sounds + a photoshoot with the Noggles glasses. Ghost has millions of fans and he digs the Noggles.

  • Indie-artist 23ETH

    • Lisa Caruso: A talented indie artist from Australia, Lisa has had her music featured on major network TV shows and has released a slew of cool music and visuals. Our plan is to create a Nounish E.P. and sound pack with her + a mini-doc following her creative process.

  • Prop House x Noun Sounds 19.5ETH

    We will be leveraging Prop House rounds for musicians to onboard new talent for songs, sounds, music videos and more. One Prop House round every month for 6 months:

    • 2ETH x 5 - Musicians will submit their music catalog and NS voters will choose (5) artists to make a new Nounish CC0 song/beat.
    • 1ETH x 5 - Musicians will submit their music catalog and be hired to create a CC0 sample pack containing loops and samples.
    • .5ETH x 4 - Anyone can submit their audio catalog and we will choose 10 people to create a new Nouns sonic logo.
    • .25ETH x 10 - Anyone can submit their audio catalog and NS holders will vote for artists to create new CC0 sound effects and real-world sounds (i.e. recording the ocean or other foley sounds)

Further Develop Nouns Sounds Platform

image1920×1046 132 KB

Part of our MVP was building out the PLAYground section of the Noun Sounds website. Building upon this, we will be creating new tools and features within the NS platform as we grow our pod:

  • Mint Playlists: The Nouns Player
    • Meet the Nouns Player companion: A free “Winamp”-style audio player that will be able to pull music from Spotify, read from the playlists and allow you to enjoy each curation.
    • Creators will be able to mint playlists of songs and podcasts from their catalogues. Taste-Makers and Curators will be able capture a moment in time and create communities around each of them.
    • This not only allows artists to create but also empowers one of the most important thing in music: curation.
    • Each Nouns Player will have the option of creating custom skins for their companion. Thus, the Playlist Creator will come along with an Album Art and “Skins” creator tool for the music player.
    • These playlists will allow creators and curators to offer token gated content (on their websites or through channels in their discords) based on each of their mintable playlists.
  • Artist Profiles
    • Artists and creators will be able to setup their own custom page to showoff their catalogue and give updates. You’ll be able to read about their work, see what packs they’re just released, or check out what playlists are currently minting.
  • Profesional Music Creator Tools
    • VST Instruments: Offering tools to experienced musicians, we will be creating professional instruments that can be used in music-making programs. From keyboards, vocals, synths, drums and more- music makers can download, install and get to making their own Noun Sounds within their existing audio workstation.
    • Soundboard: A fully functional CC0 soundboard on Noun Sounds for use across all Twitter Spaces and more. One of our most requested features.

Nounish Partnerships

image1080×675 91.8 KB

Partnering with Save The Music, we will proliferate the good vibes via IRL impacts and educational experiences. Noun Sounds will work with STM to provide funding and educational materials to their existing programs by donating Noun Sounds tokens from the founders wallet. These Noun Sounds NFTs will allow STM to submit proposals to the NS DAO for funding. Some concepts for Noun Sounds DAO proposals:

  • Video chats with Noun Sounds musicians: Kids get lessons, music talks and even song reviews/feedback from professional musicians in a 1-on-1 environment
  • Funding students to create music and sound packs for the Noun Sounds library
  • Teaching students about web3, DAOs and Nouns
  • Fund students via Prop House round

Noun Sounds DAO

image1688×576 25.8 KB

Using the Nouns Builder, Noun Sounds will create a daily auction to onboard new users into the Noun Sounds ecosystem. Holding a Noun Sounds NFT allows you to put proposals on chain, vote on proposals and have an overall voice in the direction of the Noun Sounds treasury and growth. With the injection of 100ETH into the initial Noun Sounds treasury, this provides immediate value to token holders while attracting musicians and music lovers to participate in governance. (NS founders wallet will receive 1/10 NS tokens for the first 2 years). Many other line items are needed in this process:

  • Community management in Discord
  • Social media management for Twitter, Instagram and TikTok
  • Content creation (video, motion graphics, graphic design +)
  • Live streams and interviews with musicians/music curators
  • Misc DAO management expenses including gas fees for transactions, any subscriptions needed (i.e. Twitter Blue, Adobe Suite etc.)
