TL;DR

Nouns cultural activation in LA that will showcase graffiti, breakdance and music.

image1480×987 201 KB

Nouns Jam? Tell me more!

NFT LA is scheduled to take place from March 20th to 23rd. We believe that Nouns should expand beyond the web3 niche, so we plan to make a bold cultural activation to onboard new and talented people. Our goal is to introduce Nouns to the graffiti and street dance community in LA, as we believe that Nouns can spark their creativity and bring value to the DAO.

  • We will announce Nouns Jam as a side event happening on Friday, March 24th.
  • The event will take place in a warehouse, just 6 minutes away from NFT LA’s venue. We will host around 80 people.
  • A Nouns Graffiti will be painted for the activation and will remain in the venue, which also hosts hip-hop, breakdance, and other events.
  • A DJ will play music for b-girls and b-boys to dance the night away. There will be prizes for cypher queen and cypher king.
  • We will offer swag, t-shirts, and stickers to those in attendance.
  • We will onboard artists and dancers to Nouns.
  • We will create multimedia content to share this cultural activation on social media and inspire dancers and artists to create with Nouns.
