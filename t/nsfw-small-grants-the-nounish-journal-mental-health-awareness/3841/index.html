WHY JOURNAL BOOKS?

  • A new medium that can be used to expand and introduce the NounsDAO to a new and wider audience.
  • Journaling is known to not only foster creativity, but also known to improve mental health.
  • The Nounish Journal will be made to help you prioritize your problems, fears, and concerns. Providing an opportunity for positive self-talk, and identifying negative thoughts and behaviors.
  • A good way to proliferate and movement to end the stigma against depression.
  • Provides new idea to draw the attention of the current and future artists who likes to make various type of books based on Nouns. As of now, Nouns already have series of Coloring books by Messhup, Lil Coloring book by Ilustradora, and A Comic Book Collection. If given the chance, The Nounish Journal will be added to that.
  • Lastly, Nouns had a lot of project for the community, such as beach/city cleanup, tree planting, giving out free foods, etc. I think it’s time for Nouns to also get involved with raising mental health awareness.

PHYSICAL AND DIGITAL COPY

The Nounish Journal will be made to be available Physically and Digitally. The physical copies will be distributed for free. Note: One of the purpose is proliferation and not revenue, as we are making a campaign for mental health awareness, it will be better to distribute it for free. A digital copy of the book will be provided. Anyone who likes to do the same movement in their country can use it to produce a physical book.

THE AUDIENCE

  • 100 pieces of The Nounish Journal will be given to strangers, in 3 different cities, in different ways. One is through a social experiment.
