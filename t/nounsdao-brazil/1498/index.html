tl;dr

Build a community to nounify Brazil ⌐◨-◨

Description

Create NounsDAO Brazil to connect to local artists and builders, aiming to create and proliferate Nouns in the country.
Also to be able to include those who might not be able to overcome language barriers by distributing content and organising events in the native language, Portuguese.

Language

Translation: nouns.wtf is already being independently translated by Eduardo Cruz, who I “met” at a Prop House “shark tank”. He could be retroactively paid, and this is something I’d like to ask the community about your opinion.

Awareness

  1. Nounify Brazil: start a contest on Twitter to nounify Brazilian symbols (such as Christ the redeemer, Neymar etc), offering 0.1 ETH reward to the image with most like by the contest deadline (deadline tbd).

  2. Nouns chalk graffiti: similarly to what will be done in Basel, have the noggles sprayed around the country, photographed and uploaded to Twitter. Also reward the user with most likes to foster engagement.

  3. Posters with Nouns and QR code: Similar to what has been done in HK and what will be done in Basel, place posters in cities such as São Paulo, Rio de Janeiro, Porto Alegre, Belo Horizonte e Recife. QR code will direct to nouns.wtf. Final budget for this will also include image and video capture for use on social media.

  4. Twitter spaces held in both Portuguese (to spread awareness) and English (to give this community access to guests that don’t speak Portuguese).

  5. Nouns graffiti: we are already in talks with professional graffiti artists to have noggles containing art done in São Paulo (12.33 million) and Porto Alegre (4.4 million). We are now scouting for ideal locations. Final budget for this will also include image and video capture for use on social media.

Budget

We are asking for 13 ETH, not counting the graffiti.

Budget breakdown:

  1. Image contest: 0.5 ETH (0.1 ETH each, to be repeated 5 times)
  2. Chalk graffiti contest: 0.5 ETH (0.25 ETH each, to be repeated 2 times)
  3. Posters: 6 ETH for poster print and distribution (print: R$400/100 posters or USD80/100 posters or 0.06 ETH/100 posters)
  4. Twitter spaces will be held once a week in Portuguese. Special spaces in English will be less frequent.
  5. Graffiti: prices for the graffiti itself are ca R$400/m2 (ca USD 80/10sq ft or 0.06 ETH/m2). We will have to add the prices for the location (tbd). This will be a separate proposal once we have all the details (curating artist, art, location and media capture - hello Marketing Studio for Nouns-funded Projects ).
  6. Administration of DAO’s projects and community management: 6 ETH. 1 ETH per person per month. We will have 2 people on this and the budget is for 3 months of work.

Team

UPDATE
Nouns Week Brazil

We are planning a Nouns week in October 2022 with simultaneous nounification of four capital cities from north to south of Brazil. The cities are Fortaleza (2,68 million), Rio de Janeiro (6,7 million), São Paulo (12,33 million), and Porto Alegre (4,4 million). In collaboration with @ccZero2022, the largest Brazilian web3 community on discord, we formed groups to work on each location. The groups will execute the project under the supervision of DAO’s founders.

We propose the following for Nouns Week Brazil:

  • Nouns graffiti: we are in talks with local graffiti artists to have noggles containing art done in central locations.
  • Nouns in São Paulo digital billboard: promote Nouns and NounsDAO Brazil displaying a selection of Nouns during the event.
  • Nouns chalk graffiti: have the noggles sprayed on the streets. Also, ask followers to tweet photos with specific hashtags and reward the ones with the most likes to foster engagement.
  • Posters with Nouns and QR code: place posters with a QR code that will direct to nouns.wtf.
  • Nouns stickers: print nouns stickers to put all over the cities and give out to kids.
  • 3-D printed noggles: distribute noggles to be worn by groups during Nouns Week Brazil interventions.

Final budget for Nouns Week Brazil will also include photo and video crew dedicated to document the event for use on social media. All the footage and photos are to be sent to the Marketing Studio cc0 stock.

Budget for this action:

  • Nouns graffiti: 10 ETH for graffiti art in the four capital cities (Fortaleza, Rio de Janeiro, São Paulo, and Porto Alegre).

  • Graffiti locations: 18 ETH, the cost varies considerably per location.

  • Nouns in São Paulo digital billboard: 3 ETH for renting two billboards advertising spaces for seven days.

  • Nouns chalk graffiti: 1 ETH for 10 chalk spray paints and 4 molds. Executed by groups formed in collaboration with members of @ccZero2022.

  • Chalk graffiti contest: 1 ETH (0.25 ETH for each capital city)

  • Posters: 6 ETH for 1000 Nouns poster. Placed by groups formed in collaboration with members of @ccZero2022.

  • Nouns stickers: 1 ETH (2000 stickers). Placed by groups formed in collaboration with members of @ccZero2022.

  • 3-D printed noggles: 1 ETH (around 10 noggles)

  • Photo and video crew: 6 ETH (for all cities)

  • Payment of our collaborators from @ccZero2022: 16 ETH (4 ETH payment for each work group/city)

  • Administration of DAO’s projects and community management: 54 ETH. 3 ETH per person per month. We will have 3 people on this and the budget is for 6 months of work.
