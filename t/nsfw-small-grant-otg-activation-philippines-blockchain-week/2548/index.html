PHILIPPINE BLOCKCHAIN WEEK

tl;dr = Campaigning NounsDAO at Philippine Blockchain Week

Description:
Philippines as the blockchain capital of Asia, with the aim of bringing together the whole blockchain ecosystem in the country. This blockchain week focus on connecting people from different sectors; businessmen, global speakers, builders/programmers, gamers, entrepreneurs, women leaders, students and even charitable institution representatives.

Fun facts about Philippines:

  • Most internet savy population: 73M+ internet users / 23M+ Cellular users
  • Social media engagement: Avg 9hrs/day
  • Top NFT ownership: 32% NFT owners (~35.2M population holding NFTs)
  • Blockchain user: 4.3M+ crypto asset owner/users

Location:
Manila, Philippines
28th Nov - 4th Dec 2022
Marriot Grand Ballroom At Newport World Resorts

Engagement points:

1. Photo Opportunity with Noggles
Physical 3D printed Noggles will be created allowing people to use them for selfies or even full body shots, nounifying their photos where they are able to share it on their social media. Emphasizing virality as well as brand awareness ⌐◨-◨

Photos from past activation for reference: Usage of 3D printed Noggles

2. Nounish Merchandise
Pins and stickers will be given out as token of participation to people who spend time engaging and interacting with Nouns. POAP will also be distributed for this activation at Philippines Block Chain week.

  • Noggle Enamel Pins
  • NSFW Stickers
  • Nouns Center Stickers
  • Noggle Stickers

Each stickers will also come with a designated QR code that direct users to a specify directory (Nouns Center, NSFW, Discourse, NounsDAO) aiding newcomers into Nouns ecosystem.

3. Gorillas Marketing
Being mobile and OTG we serve as a walking billboard promoting/invoke curiosity about Nouns/Noggle. Stickers double up as merch-on-the-go where we are able to engage in sticker bombing/tagging.
