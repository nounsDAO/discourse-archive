SMALL GRANTS: NOUNISH Potted Plants

elle

PROPOSAL: NOUNISH POTTED PLANTS

TL;DR
Requesting for 3ETH to set up a Nounish pop up store; display and sale of Nounish potted plants.

PROJECT OVERVIEW / WHY
The objective and inspiration for this project is the idea of exploring ways to proliferate Noun branding as well as reaching out to more non-crypto native users via lifestyle items thus; Nounish potted plants were born.

Using 3D printing technology, pots will be designed in a nounish way that not only represent the brand but looks fun and functional. Nounish potted plants serve as a activation, as the main goal is to create a series of Nounish lifestyle items.

BUDGET / FUNDS BREAKDOWN

  • Nounish Pop-up Store (0.7ETH)
  • Pop-up Store Operation (0.3ETH)
  • Retro Funding (0.5ETH)
  • Production (1.5ETH)

1. Nounish Pop-up Store.
To set up a pop-up store at prime shopping location at Singapore (Either SCAPE, Gardens By The Bay, Esplanade, etc); to display and sale of Nounish potted plants.
Duration: 2-3 days (Fri, Sat & Sunday)

2. Pop-up Store Operation
To manage operation, promote Nounish potted plants as well as Nouns DAO to visitors.
