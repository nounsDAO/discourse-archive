Retire and Redesign traits after # of Occurrences

0xmummy

Some noun heads are beginning to become redundant or attached to a specific holders. In a fun way to create more variations, I’m thinking we retire some heads and replace with color variations. ie. new Skateboard, Wizard hat, skully hats.

A system could be set up so every 400 nouns (~1year), we retire/replace heads if they have between 5-10 in supply at the time. This would result in new demand from existing holders wanting to collect a “set”, and new holders that would like a more unique identity (ie. Gami = Green Skateboard).

A competition could go out to designers to have new variations made, as well as a vote for heads that the community should deem “untouchable”.
