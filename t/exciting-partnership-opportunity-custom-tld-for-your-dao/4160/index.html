Hello Nouns community!

I am reaching out as the co-founder of the W3UP Project with an exciting partnership opportunity that we believe would greatly benefit your DAO and its members.

Imagine having a TLD like “.nouns” or even something more unique and eye-catching like “.⌐◨-◨”! This would not only strengthen your brand identity but also provide an additional revenue stream for the DAO, as we propose a 50-50 split of the minting fee.

You can visit https://uni.w3up.cc/ to mint your own “.uni” domain and test our platform. This example was specifically chosen to celebrate Uniswap’s arrival on the Binance Smart Chain.

For more information on this partnership, please review our documentation here: W3Up DAO-NFT Partnership

We are excited about the potential of this partnership and would love to discuss it further with your community. If you have any questions or suggestions, please feel free to reach out to us. We’re looking forward to hearing your thoughts and exploring this opportunity together!
