[Small grants]: Nounish t-shirts for any occasion

fiveoceans_dev

I want to make a big batch of t-shirts using graphical content created by NounsDAO community. The t-shirts will be light green and made of cotton in South Korea. The t-shirts will be sold online at a minimum price of $14-15

The money from sales will be used to make more t-shirts. The goal of the proposal is to provide quality t-shirt to nounish community and test grounds for a more sustainable model of t-shirt business.

Design
At least 20 different designs based on graphics produced by the Nouns community.

Sticker placement (3 stickers)

  • a big graphic on chest part of the t-shirt
  • a small logo of red noggles on left side of chest
  • a small logo of mural on left shoulder

Budget:
1 eth - website store integrated with PayPal and Stripe
1 eth - packaging and logistics
8 eth - manufacturing t-shirts (S, M, L, XL sizes; at least 20 designs)

  • The estimated cost per one t-shirt is $14
  • with 9 eth we can make around 800 t-shirts

Total: 10 ETH

Timeline 1 month ± 1 week
1 week website dev
1 week pre-order
1 week manufacturing
1 week shipping
