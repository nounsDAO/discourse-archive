Small Grants: Nouns DAO Children’s Books Project

0xrel

TLDR: Creating 3 children’s books with Nouns DAO characters and donating 100 books to an orphanage.

Description:
Nouns DAO has gained popularity in the blockchain and crypto community due to its innovative organizational model and unique usage compared to traditional organizations. However, few people are aware of the Nouns DAO characters and their involvement in society. Therefore, this proposal aims to develop Nouns DAO characters and expand their reach through children’s books.

Proposal:
This proposal seeks a small grant of 0.6 ETH to produce 3 children’s books featuring Nouns DAO characters. The books will include short stories with illustrations and text that are easy for children aged 2-7 to understand. Additionally, 100 books will be donated to an orphanage to improve children’s cognitive abilities and provide positive benefits to society.

Benefits:
By creating children’s books featuring Nouns DAO characters, we can introduce Nouns DAO to the public and expand its reach. This also provides an opportunity to connect Nouns DAO with positive values such as children’s education and provide positive benefits to society. As an organization that cares about the community, this activity will enhance the image of Nouns DAO as an organization that cares about the community.
